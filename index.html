Here Are Some Emerging Technology for Cybersecurity.

Zero Trust Technology

It is an upcoming security model that requires identitity verification for every user on a device that attempts to connect to a organizations private resources. The idea of the model is to believe that anyone is to not be trusted and is designed to have strict and secure boundaries of what a user can do.
